## HISTORICAL PERIOD SEARCH STRATEGY

### Colonial Period (pre-1975)
- **Approach**: Portuguese colonial medical records and archives
- **Sources**: Historical archives, missionary hospital records, colonial administration
- **Challenges**: Limited digital availability, language barriers

### Post-Independence Transition (1975-1985)
- **Approach**: Early surveillance system documentation, WHO assistance records
- **Sources**: WHO archives, early government health reports, international assistance
- **Challenges**: Civil war disruption, limited institutional capacity

### Civil War Period (1975-2002)
- **Approach**: Humanitarian organization reports, refugee health data, conflict-affected health
- **Sources**: UN agencies, MSF, IRC, UNHCR health reports
- **Challenges**: Access limitations, fragmented reporting

### Modern Surveillance Era (2002-present)
- **Approach**: IDSR implementation, WHO AFRO collaboration, academic literature
- **Sources**: Official surveillance, peer-reviewed research, international partnerships
- **Advantages**: Better documentation, digital availability
